About PPM

Local NJ property management,
built differently

We started PPM because NJ landlords deserved better

There are two kinds of property management companies in New Jersey.

The first is the large franchise operation. They have a name you recognize and a call center you'll get routed through every time you have a question about your own property.

The second is the solo operator — a landlord who started managing a few other people's rentals on the side, figuring things out as they go.

Proactive Property Management was founded in 2023 to be a third option. Professionally run. Locally rooted. Small enough to know your property by name, built well enough to handle anything NJ throws at it.

Who we are

DR
Dan Rivera — Founder & Principal
BS Finance, Cornell University · NJ Real Estate

Dan holds a BS in Finance from Cornell University and has spent over a decade active in New Jersey real estate — as an investor, a licensed agent, and a property manager. He's been on both sides of the landlord-tenant relationship and built PPM with that perspective: if you're going to hand someone the keys to your investment, they should treat it the way they'd treat their own.

PPM runs on AI-powered property management systems — which means maintenance gets routed faster, owner reports are always current, and the things that typically fall through the cracks don't. One management fee covers it. No surprise charges, no maintenance markups.

How we work

One point of contact

You're not calling a general line. You have a direct number for the person who knows your property.

AI-powered, human-accountable

Automated systems handle rent collection, maintenance dispatching, owner reporting, and lease generation. A real person is responsible for the outcome.

NJ compliance, built in

New Jersey has some of the strictest landlord-tenant laws in the country — rent control in over a dozen municipalities, specific security deposit rules, just-cause eviction requirements. We stay current so our owners don't have to.

What you see is what you pay

One management fee. No setup charges. No technology fees. No markup on contractor invoices.
