Services

Everything. So you don't have to.

Full-service residential property management for NJ landlords and investors. One flat management fee covers everything below.

Tenant Placement

Marketing, showings, thorough screening — income, credit, background, and rental history. We fill vacancies with the right tenants.

  • Listing across Zillow, Realtor.com, Apartments.com, and more
  • Professional photography and listing copy
  • Income verification (3x monthly rent standard)
  • Credit check, background check, eviction history
  • Prior landlord reference checks
  • NJ-compliant lease execution and move-in inspection

Rent Collection

Automated online collection with direct owner disbursements. You get paid on time, every time.

  • Online tenant payment portal (ACH and card)
  • Automated late fee enforcement per NJ law
  • Direct owner disbursements
  • Real-time transaction visibility in owner portal
  • Monthly reconciliation and statement

Maintenance Coordination

24/7 tenant maintenance requests, trusted vendor network, and owner approval on significant repairs.

  • 24/7 tenant maintenance request handling
  • Vendor network across Northern & Central NJ
  • Owner approval on repairs above stated threshold
  • Documented work orders and vendor invoices
  • No markup on contractor costs — you see the actual invoice
  • Preventive maintenance scheduling

Financial Reporting

Monthly statements and 24/7 portal access. A clear picture of your property's performance at all times.

  • Monthly owner statements
  • Annual summary for tax preparation
  • 24/7 owner portal access (Buildium)
  • Rent roll and expense tracking
  • Year-end 1099 preparation

Lease Management

NJ-compliant lease preparation, renewals, and enforcement. We stay current on every municipality's requirements.

  • NJ-compliant lease drafting
  • Lease renewal management and negotiation
  • Rent increase notices per NJ law
  • Lease violation notices and enforcement
  • Rent control compliance where applicable

Legal Compliance

NJ landlord-tenant law is complex. We handle compliance so you don't have to.

  • Required landlord disclosures (Truth in Renting, flood zone, lead paint)
  • Rent control registration and compliance
  • Habitability standards monitoring
  • Eviction coordination when necessary
  • Ongoing NJ law monitoring and owner updates
