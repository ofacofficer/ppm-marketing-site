Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Last reviewed: July 8, 2026

Our commitment

Proactive Property Management ("PPM") is committed to making njpropertymanager.com usable by everyone, including people who use assistive technology such as screen readers, screen magnification, voice control, or keyboard-only navigation. Our goal is conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and we treat accessibility as an ongoing responsibility rather than a one-time project — we continue to test, fix, and improve as the site evolves.

We do not claim this site is fully or perfectly accessible at every moment. Websites change often, and no automated or manual review catches everything. What we can commit to is a good-faith, continuing effort toward conformance, and a clear way to reach us when something falls short.

What we've done

  • Structured pages with semantic HTML landmarks (header, nav, main, footer) and a logical heading order.
  • Provided a "Skip to content" link at the top of every page for keyboard users.
  • Written meaningful alt text for content images and marked purely decorative graphics so screen readers skip them.
  • Reviewed and adjusted text and background color combinations sitewide to meet the WCAG AA contrast minimums (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text and UI components).
  • Added a visible focus outline for keyboard navigation across interactive elements.
  • Associated form fields (like the rental-analysis and contact forms) with visible or programmatic labels.
  • Used underlines or other non-color cues on links that appear inside body text, so links don't rely on color alone.

Known limitations

Some parts of the experience are outside our direct control, including the third-party owner and tenant login portal (hosted by our property-management software provider) and embedded map or review widgets. We can't guarantee the accessibility of third-party services we link to, but we choose reputable vendors and will raise issues with them when we learn of one. If you encounter a barrier anywhere on this site — including third-party portals we link to — please let us know using the contact information below.

Feedback and contact

If you have trouble accessing any part of this website, or you use assistive technology and hit a barrier, please contact us:

Email: contact@njppm.com
Phone: (973) 330-8261

Please include the web address (URL) of the page and a description of the issue. We aim to acknowledge accessibility reports within 2 business days and to provide a plan or fix within a reasonable timeframe based on the complexity of the issue.

Scope

This statement applies to njpropertymanager.com. It does not cover third-party sites we link to, such as our owner/tenant portal provider or social media platforms.
