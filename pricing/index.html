Pricing questions

Do you charge based on rent collected or rent due?

Collected. We only earn our fee when you get paid. If a tenant doesn't pay, we don't collect our management fee for that month.

What's the leasing fee?

The one-time leasing fee covers finding and placing a new tenant — listing, marketing, showings, screening, and lease execution. The exact amount depends on your property and location. We'll quote it clearly upfront.

Do you mark up maintenance costs?

No. You see the actual vendor invoice. PPM does not add a percentage on top of repair costs.

Is there a minimum portfolio size?

No minimum. We work with landlords who own one rental unit as well as investors managing larger portfolios.

Can I cancel the management agreement?

Month-to-month contracts are available. Ask us for details on our agreement structure.
