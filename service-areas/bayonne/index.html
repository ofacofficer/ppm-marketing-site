The Bayonne rental market

Bayonne sits on a peninsula between Jersey City and Staten Island, with the Hudson-Bergen Light Rail and quick access to the Holland Tunnel and the Turnpike. It is one of Hudson County's more attainable rental markets — heavy on two- and three-family homes and converted rowhouses — and draws commuters priced out of Hoboken and Downtown Jersey City. Waterfront redevelopment around Cape Liberty keeps adding newer inventory.

The bread-and-butter here is small multi-family: duplexes, triplexes, and modest walk-ups. Tenant placement, fast turnovers, and upkeep of older stock are where day-to-day management matters most.
